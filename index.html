Open Design Thesis

Improving the design of Electronic Health Records

Who:

I’m Eliot, a master’s student at VUW. I’ve decided to document my entire thesis process online, and make it open source.

What are you designing:

Electronic Health Record Software. When you go to a doctors appointment, a GP will take notes about your health, and store them in EHR software. Despite the incredible importance of this software, it's design is lacking, and hopefully I can improve it.

Why open source:

EHR systems appear to work more efficiently if they’re widely adopted. Therefore to give my project it’s best chance it must be open. Also, the open source movement is very focused on code, it will be an interesting challenge making an open design project.
